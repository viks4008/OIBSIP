Lata Mangeshkar

Sept 1929 - Feb 2022

Biography

Lata Mangeshkar born Hema Mangeshkar 28 September 1929 - 6 February 2022 was an Indian playback singer. She is considered to be one of the greatest singers of the Indian subcontinent. Her voice was one of the unifying elements of the people of South Asia across borders. Her contribution to the Indian music industry in a career spanning eight decades gained her honorific titles such as the "Queen of Melody", "Nightingale of India", and "Voice of the Millennium".
